Gnocchi

Description

Gnocchi is simple to make with just three ingredients: mashed potato, flour, and egg. This recipe is used for generations.

Ingredients

  1. Potatoes
  2. flour
  3. Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Combine the ingredients, then knead into a ball.
  3. Shape the dough into "snakes."
  4. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  5. Boil and drain the gnocchi.
